About California

The Southern California coast is part of the western coastline of the United States and spans from Point Conception in the north to the United States-Mexico border in the south. The region generally experiences a Mediterranean climate characterized by mild, wet winters and warm, dry summers. Southern Califonia also has some great beaches! Popular ones include Venice Beach, Santa Monica Beach, Huntington Beach, and Malibu.
